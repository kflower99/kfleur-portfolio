About

Creative Research Journey

K. Fleur is an interdisciplinary artist, sound practitioner, and cultural architect with 15 years building immersive installations, spatial audio environments, and cultural platforms at the intersection of Afro-Caribbean diasporic research and emerging technology.

She holds an MFA from UCLA Design Media Arts, where she was an Alissa Bierra Graduate Fellow in Black Feminist Research, and a VR Storytelling Fellowship from UNCSA. Her work has been supported by the California Arts Council, Rauschenberg/NYFA, and the American Craft Council, among others.
